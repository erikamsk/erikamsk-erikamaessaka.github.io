Sunweb App: Personalizing the Booking Experience


UX Design

▷ View Prototype

(Project created in 2020)

Native App Redesign

Redesign proposal for a new Sunweb App. The application offers a complete experience to travellers of the Dutch travel agency, from the booking until the return flight. Users can organise their trips, book excursions and chat with their Sunweb travel agent. Another differential is the curated content based on the user’s past trips and interests, with personalised recommendations for destinations, best fitting hotels and excursions.


The project was created entirely using Adobe XD.
